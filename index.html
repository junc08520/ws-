Selected Research & Articles

1. Deep Neural Networks for Short-Term Stock Forecasting

Abstract: We evaluate recurrent and convolutional architectures on 10 years of intraday and daily price data. Results show improved short-term directional accuracy when combining price features with volume and volatility indicators.

Authors: J. Li, M. Patel — Journal of Financial Data Science, 2023. Read full paper

2. Sentiment Signals from News & Social Media

Abstract: This study uses transformer-based NLP to extract sentiment at sentence-level from financial news and social feeds. Incorporating sentiment improves short-horizon volatility forecasts across multiple sectors.

Source: Proceedings of Computational Finance 2022. Read more

3. Reinforcement Learning for Portfolio Rebalancing

Abstract: A comparative analysis of policy gradient methods for allocation decisions under transaction costs. The RL agent shows stable outperformance versus simple heuristics in simulated markets.

Authors: S. Rivera et al. — Quantitative Finance Letters, 2024. Read more

4. Feature Engineering for Alpha Discovery

Abstract: Discusses techniques to construct predictive signals from price, fundamental ratios, and order flow. Emphasis on cross-validation, leakage prevention, and robust evaluation.

Source: University Research Working Paper, 2021. Download

5. Cross-Market Correlation Analysis: Crypto vs. Equities

Abstract: Investigates lead-lag relationships between major cryptocurrencies and technology stocks, highlighting times of increased co-movement during macro shocks.

Published: Market Microstructure Research, 2023. Read more

6. Explainable AI in Finance: SHAP & Model Interpretability

Abstract: Presents best practices for applying SHAP values to tree and neural models to generate transparent feature attributions for portfolio managers.

Conference: Explainable AI for Finance, 2022. Details

7. Backtesting Frameworks and Robust Evaluation

Abstract: Covers pitfalls in backtesting including survivorship bias, lookahead bias, and proper transaction cost modeling to ensure realistic performance estimates.

Source: Quantitative Methods Journal, 2020. Read paper

8. Macro-Factor Driven Asset Allocation

Abstract: Evaluates multi-factor allocation strategies that adapt to regime changes using macroeconomic indicators and AI regime detection.

Source: Journal of Asset Management, 2021. Read more

9. Volatility Forecasting with GARCH and Machine Learning Hybrids

Abstract: Compares parametric GARCH models with ML regressors for realized volatility forecasting and intraday risk estimation.

Authors: K. Zhou et al., 2019. Details

10. Practical Guide to Feature Selection in Financial Models

Abstract: A tutorial-style article on recursive feature elimination, L1 regularization, and domain-driven feature creation for robust predictive models.

Blog / Tutorial, 2022. Read tutorial
